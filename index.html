Welcome to Interactive Filmmaking, where you're not just a viewer, but a key player in the creative process! Our website features an engaging, interactive video that takes you behind the scenes of our movie-making adventure. As you watch, you'll encounter choices that impact the storyline, the characters' actions, and even the outcome of the film. Whether you're deciding on the next scene, choosing the perfect location, or guiding the actors' performances, your decisions shape the movie's journey.
